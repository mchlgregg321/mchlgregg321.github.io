Our Company

About Highline Stoneworks

Highline Stoneworks is a family-owned masonry company proudly serving Bolivar, MO, and surrounding communities throughout The Ozarks and southwest Missouri. With a passion for timeless design and structural integrity, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional stonework that stands the test of time.

We specialize in custom patios, retaining walls, fireplaces, stone entryways, stone facades, and premium countertop installations. Our comprehensive approach covers every aspect of your project—from material sourcing and fabrication to meticulous installation and finishing touches.

The company has forged strong relationships with stone suppliers and manufacturers, striving to source quality materials and provide innovative stone solutions for the evolving construction market. Based in Bolivar and serving The Ozarks region, Highline Stoneworks upholds exceptional standards of quality and detail in all our offerings.

Our team brings a deep understanding of the intricacies involved in both residential and commercial stone projects. We manage the entire process from material selection to manufacturing and installation, ensuring meticulous oversight and quality control at every step.
