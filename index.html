About Us

Precision. Integrity. Craftsmanship.

Highline Stoneworks is a family-owned masonry company proudly serving Bolivar, MO, and surrounding communities. With a passion for timeless design and structural integrity, we specialize in custom patios, retaining walls, fireplaces, entryways, and stone facades. Every project is guided by our commitment to honest work, transparent communication, and stunning results.
