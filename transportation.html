Getting Around Taniti

Public Buses

Taniti’s affordable public bus system is a convenient way to travel between towns and local attractions.

Taxi Service

Taxis are available in the capital city and at major hotels. They offer quick and private transportation across the island.

Car Rentals

Prefer to explore at your own pace? Rent a car from the airport or city center and enjoy the freedom to roam.
