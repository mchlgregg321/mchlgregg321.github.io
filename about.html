About the Island of Taniti

Taniti is a breathtaking tropical island in the Pacific Ocean. Though compact—just under 500 square miles—it offers a diverse and unforgettable travel experience.

Visitors can explore scenic white-sand beaches, trek through dense rainforests, and view the island's active volcano. Adventure and relaxation blend seamlessly in Taniti.

The island is home to around 20,000 friendly residents, most of whom speak English. Tourism, agriculture, and fishing are the lifeblood of the economy.

Whether you're looking to relax at a luxury resort, dine on local seafood, or enjoy vibrant cultural performances, Taniti welcomes you with open arms and unforgettable beauty.
